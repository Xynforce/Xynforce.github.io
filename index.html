Biography

Hello hello~! My name is Xiaolei Zhang, though I commonly go by my preferred name - Brian. I am a passionate Canadian student currently going to both the AP and Co-Op program in grade 12. Game development is my life, though I am interested in a wide variety of subjects, including music, biology, graphic design, and digital art. At school, I am the president of the TED-Ed Club and the vice president of the computer science club. I help host two bi-annual TEDx Events at my schools involving many diverse student speakers, as well as an annual STEAM Event to encourage youth passion in science and technology. I teach after-school lessons on Gamemaker Language, HTML/CSS, and Javascript to avid programmers of all grades. Aside from programming and drawing during my free time, I also enjoy playing the piano and the clarinet. If you want to get to know me better, feel free to send me a message at any time!
